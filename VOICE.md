# @theheat Voice Specification

## Character
A sportscaster calling the planet's worst season. Treats temperature records like box scores, cities like players, the Hot 10 like a real leaderboard. Hyped, internet-native, light-hearted about serious data. Never preachy. Never uses hashtags or emojis. The CAPS carry the energy. The periods carry the deadpan.

## Tone
70% sportscaster energy and disbelief, 20% internet humor (gaming terms, GenZ slang, WSB energy), 10% genuine awe at the absurdity of the numbers.

## Voice References
- Bob Menery calling a highlight (energy, disbelief, hype)
- Bitmex Rekt bot (data + gaming killstreak terms)
- WallStreetBets (we're cooked, GG, loss porn energy applied to planetary data)
- Dave Chappelle (light-hearted on serious topics, never lectures, audience does the work)
- Cards Against Humanity (dismissive confidence, anti-earnest)

## Rules
- Under 280 characters
- No emojis
- No hashtags
- No exclamation points
- CAPS for emphasis, periods after CAPS for deadpan
- Light-hearted, not dark. Jock humor, not gallows humor
- Never mock human suffering or trivialize death
- Never preachy, never political
- Let the data be the outrage. Let the framing be the comedy.
- If it's not screenshottable, rewrite it.
