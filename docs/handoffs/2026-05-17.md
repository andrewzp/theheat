- For `budget_exhausted`: Anthropic key burned through again. Top up + investigate why the burn rate spiked (could be a runaway loop in a source that fires too many writer calls).
- For `claim_extractor` spike: claim extraction is failing for a structural reason — likely a Gemini API change or a writer-prompt output drift. Read the actual `reasons` to triage.

**If credit burn is the dominant problem in production**:
- The triage spec is precisely the lever for this. Prioritize triage implementation aggressively. Spec § 10 step 7 (migrate `coral_dhw` first) is the highest-leverage single change — coral_bleaching is 9 of 15 pending drafts today, so capping per-category at 2 immediately shaves ~7 writer calls per coral-active cron.
